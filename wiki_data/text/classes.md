# Classes

**Classes** are an integral property of adventuring cats, which apply stat modifiers and determine which abilities are offered from post combat rewards. Newborn cats start out Collarless, but may become a different class by assigning the corresponding collar (if it is unlocked) when beginning a new adventure. If no collar is assigned, the cat will receive no stat modifiers and will only be offered Collarless abilities which are offered to all cats.

Initially, only five classes are available: Collarless, Fighter, Hunter, Mage, and Tank. Additional classes are unlocked by beating areas for the first time and then talking to Butch, who will award that area's corresponding collar.

## Trivia

* Many Mini-Bosses represent certain classes.
  + Magnus   Magnus  Has a chain dash and spin attack that hits all adjacent units!  represents the Fighter
  + Fenrir   Fenrir  Tosses bear traps!   
     Has an affinity for tall grass.   
     Knocks back Melee attackers.  represents the Hunter
  + Maisie   Maisie  Protects her kittens when they are attacked.  represents the Tank
  + Lucy   Lucy  Her powerful spells take time to charge up.  represents the Mage
  + Marshmallow   Marshmallow  Her lick attack Charms units.  represents the Cleric
  + Dack   Dack  His ranged attacks scatter held coins.  
    Has a melee attack that inflicts Poison.  
    Seeks coin pickups.  
    Gains a Backflip every time he collects a coin.  represents the Thief
  + Morana   Morana  Its ranged attack inflicts leech.   
     Reanimates Dead Bodies.   
     Revives through dead bodies.  represents the Necromancer
  + Gein   Gein  *Hooks and pulls units that end their movement in range, inflicting Bleed.*  
    *Does a melee spin attack that spawns meat.*  
    *Seeks meat pickups.*  
    *Trample.*  represents the Butcher
  + Alice   Alice  Has a psychic choke attack that inflicts Bruise on a unit in her line of sight.  
     Knocks back any enemy within her line of sight that casts a spell.  represents the Psychic
  + Draven   Draven  Heals and buffs his crows.  
     Enters Squirrel Form once his crows die.  represents the Druid
  + Franklin   Franklin  Spawns robots and crafts weapons. Enters a Mech when at low HP.  represents the Tinkerer
  + Chan Hung   Chan Hung  Uses his own actions every time one of his enemies uses an action.  
     Has a different response for move, attack/weapon, and spell/trinket.  represents the Monk
  + Arthur   Arthur  Who knows what he will do!?  represents the Jester
  + Pebbles   Pebbles  Its melee attack knocks units backwards.   
     Can throw rocks.  represents the Collarless despite being considered a Boss and not a Mini-Boss.
  + Rocky Bobo   Rocky Bobo  Moves towards what damages it. Does a body slam attack in the direction he's facing. Can levitate rocks!  also represents the Collarless
* Spun Cat   Spun Cat  Has a random class basic attack that inflicts a randomly assigned debuff.  has class themed variants for every class except for the Jester
